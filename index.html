About Me

I’m Dania Alnahdi, a student from Bahrain pursuing a BS in Computer Science and Design in Boston, MA. I’m interested in web and software development, inclusive and human-centered technology, and interactive (and pretty) user interfaces.

Thanks to my coursework at Northeastern University, experience at MFS and ManageUN, and the numerious internet rabbit holes I tumble into, I’m always exploring new skills from both the development and design worlds.

When I’m not behind my computer, you’ll likely find me advocating for causes I’m passionate about, or searching for a cat to pet.
